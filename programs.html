What We Do

Our Programs

Creating opportunities and empowering boys through impactful programs.

🎨 Dance, Arts & Crafts

We promote creativity and self-expression through arts and crafts. Boys are encouraged to explore their talents, build confidence, and express their emotions through creativity.

📚 Education Enrichment

We support literacy, numeracy, and learning development. Our goal is to inspire a love for education and equip boys with the tools they need to succeed academically.

🛠️ Vocational Training

We provide practical skills such as carpentry, agriculture, tailoring and other trades to help boys become self-sufficient and financially independent.

💻 Computer Literacy

We bridge the digital gap by teaching basic computer skills such as typing, internet usage and software tools necessary in today's world.

🩺 Health & Wellness

We promote physical and mental well-being through health education, hygiene awareness, and community wellness programs.

🤝 Boy Mom & Son Initiative

We bring mothers and sons together to support vulnerable boys. This program builds strong communities and mentorship connections.
