Empowering boys. Transforming lives.

Building responsible, confident and independent gentlemen.

Ntodeni Nenungwi Foundation supports less privileged boys from rural areas in Limpopo through education, mentorship, skills development and wellness programs.

Donate Now Explore Programs

Our Mission

To raise boys to become responsible men who can take care of themselves and their families.

Our Vision

To protect the boy child from harmful influences and make the transition into manhood less difficult.

Our Focus

Education, mentorship, computer literacy, vocational training, health, wellness and community support.

About NNF

Giving the boy child an equal chance to succeed.

NNF is a non-profit organisation focused on helping boys from disadvantaged communities. We provide resources and support to help them grow into socially conscious and financially independent young men.
