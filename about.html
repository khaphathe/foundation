About Us

We believe every boy deserves a fair chance.

Ntodeni Nenungwi Foundation is a non-profit organisation focused on helping less privileged boys from rural areas in Limpopo. The foundation provides resources, guidance and support throughout their educational journey, whether formal or vocational.


Our ultimate goal is to help the boy child become socially conscious, responsible and financially independent.

Our Vision

To protect the boy child from harmful influences and support the journey into manhood.

Our Mission

To raise boys to become responsible men who can take care of themselves and their families.

Founder & Chairman

Ntodeni Shadrack Nenungwi

Ntodeni Shadrack Nenungwi is a highly accomplished physiotherapist with a BSc degree in Physiotherapy. He manages multiple successful private practices and serves as the chairman of NNF.


His leadership is driven by a passion for uplifting and empowering young boys from underprivileged backgrounds. He is also an author and devoted father.

Together, we can change lives.

Join us in empowering boys and creating a brighter future for communities in Limpopo.
